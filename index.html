WEB

Web Editing nowadays can be a variety of things, it ranges from simple content editing to media editing, from web analytics to website maintenance, implementing or editing code in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, making templates, optimalisation for different screens and so forth. A webmaster has to be a jack of all traders, able to communicate projects or problems to developpers or graphic designers, or doing it all him or herself. Here's a brief summary of what i can do for you.

Website Management

I can manage any website CMS or modify pages individually, by using ftp-methods. These tasks include user management, editing pages, creating forms or managing the site-structure and menus to optimize UX.

Google Analytics

I can implement Google Analytics in your website and keep track of the website's activity and traffic. And custom daily, weekly or monthly reports can be sent to an e-mailadress of your choice.

Social Media

Your online presence can be enhanced by setting up various Social Media channels, while setting up a brand or corporate identity: logo, cover, descripton, linking them together... Furthermore, I can develop a communication plan for each channel and schedule posts for each channel.

Web Development

As a Web Editor, I can create single page websites and online apps. I can also edit any HTML, CSS, Javascript, jQuery, PHP or work with Sass. And I understand Command Line and Git.

PRINT

Print Editing is the editing of all that is print: books, magazines, posters and flyers, and each medium requires an individual approach. I have experience with most of these print forms and am also acquainted with printer and lay-outer slang and issues. In total, I have edited over a hundred books in various forms: from paperback novels to children's pop-up books, science books, school books, comic books, co-productions with foreign editors etc. Here are a few titles I am most proud of:

GRAPHIC
