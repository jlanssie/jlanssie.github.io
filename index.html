WEB

Web Editing nowadays is a variety of things, it ranges from simple content editing to media editing, from web analytics to website maintenance, implementing or editing code in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, making templates, optimalisation for different screens and so forth. A webmaster has to be a jack of all traders, able to communicate projects or problems to developpers or graphic designers, or doing it all him or herself. Here's a brief summary of what I can do for you.

Website Management

I can manage any website CMS or modify static pages by using ftp-methods or Git. This includes user management, editing pages, creating forms or managing the site-structure and menus to optimize UX.

Google Analytics

I can implement Google Analytics in your website and keep track of the website's activity and traffic. And custom daily, weekly or monthly reports can be sent to an e-mailadress of your choice.

Social Media

I can enhance your online presence by setting up various Social Media channels and developping a brand or corporate identity: logo, cover, descripton, linking them together... Furthermore, I can develop a communication plan for each channel and manage or schedule its activiy.

Web Development

As a Web Editor, I can create single page websites and online apps. I can also edit any HTML, CSS, Javascript, jQuery, PHP or work with Sass. And I understand Command Line and Git.

PRINT

Print Editing is the editing of all that is print: books, magazines, posters and flyers, and each medium requires an individual approach. I have experience with most of these print forms and can edit any print medium. I am also acquainted with printer and lay-outer slang and issues. In total, I have edited over a hundred books in various forms: from paperback novels to children's pop-up books, science books, school books, comic books, co-productions with foreign editors etc. Here are a few titles I am most proud of:

GRAPHIC

As Mr Y., I create digital illustrations and collages. You can find them on Facebook. But my work as a graphic editor is not limited to the Mr Y. creations. As a Print Editor for WPG Uitgevers België, I was responsible for text and graphics of all Children's Books and Young Adult Literature by Manteau, Oogappel and Standaard Uitgeverij. And as a webmaster for La Monnaie, I was responsible for all the images on Lamonnaie.be. I can edit any photo, brief graphic designers, make sketches, make collages or illustrate logos.
