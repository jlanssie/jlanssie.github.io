PRINT

A long time ago, in a galaxy far, far away... It is a period of editorial war. Rebel books, striking from a hidden printer, have won their first victory against the evil Digital Empire. During the battle, Rebel books managed to be read, and they revealed to readers the secret plans to the Empire's ultimate weapon, the INTERNET, an armored digital station with enough power to destroy an entire library. Pursued by the Empire's sinister agents, Prince Jeremy races home aboard a classic novel, custodian of the stolen plans that can save the people and restore eloquence in the galaxy...
