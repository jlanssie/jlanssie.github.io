Webdev

I had my first digital experience during my journalism MA. It started out with some vanilla stuff: Wordpress and some embedding and adapting basic code. But soon after, while working for Antwerp-based journal, I got xhtml shoved down my throat. And before I knew what was going on, I started using the whip on html and css at a financial newspaper. I was still getting dominated by JavaScript and PHP, but nowadays, I'm in control. At this moment, I'm a webmaster at an opera house. In my free time, I'm working on or pimping some of my own projects. If you like to watch, take a look at my codepens. Or take a glance at my Github repositories
