A webmaster has to be a jack of all traders, able to communicate projects or problems to developpers or graphic designers, or doing it all him or herself. Here's a brief summary of what i can do for you.

I can manage any website CMS or modify pages individually, by using ftp-methods. These tasks include user management, editing pages, creating forms or managing the site-structure and menus to optimize UX.
