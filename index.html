Webdev

I had my first digital experience during my journalism MA. It started out with some Vanilla Wordpress and some embedding and adapting stuff. But soon after, while working for Antwerp-based journal, I got xhtml shoved down my throat. And before I knew what was going on, I started using the whip on html and css at a financial newspaper. And right now, I'm a webmaster at an opera house. In my free time, I'm working on or pimping some of my own projects.
